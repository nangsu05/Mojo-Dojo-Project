POOLS

Welcome to the tranquil haven of Villa De Amore, where our pool zone is nothing short of a paradise. We take pride in offering not just one, but two stunning pools to ensure your utmost relaxation and enjoyment.

Its unique sloping beach entry exudes elegance, while the built-in seating is tailored for unparalleled comfort. Every moment in this poolside paradise whispers of exclusivity and indulgence. Join us, and immerse yourself in a world where luxury knows no bounds.

GARDEN

The garden of Villa de Amore is an elegantly designed sanctuary that spans over 500 square meters, enveloped by white walls that are a seamless extension of the villa itself. Crafted by a renowned landscape team, the garden is a harmonious blend of hardscape, softscape, vegetation, and lighting, all meticulously arranged to create an atmosphere conducive to relaxation and true repose.

At the heart of the hardscape is a doublelayered circular patio with the upper circle decked in wood, setting the stage for speeches, live music, or as a gathering space that radiates a welcoming vibe. The lower circle features polished stones topped with pristine white gravel, offering as tunning backdrop for photos, especially with the romantic LED lighting that lines the base of both circles, enhancing the nocturnal charm. Surrounding this focal point are polished stone benches and integrated planters, completed with waterproof power outlets for added convenience. Pathways to this central area are paved with large, angular dark grey slabs that join seamlessly with the larger washed gravel pieces near the house, all bearing the distinctive Villa de Amore logo.
The softscape and plant arrangements are thoughtfully curated, with layers of vegetation creating a picturesque setting for our esteemed guests. The lush green lawn is fitted with an irrigation system and is regularly maintained to ensure its vibrant appearance. The Villa de Amore team and their softscape partners have handpicked each plant from across Thailand to ensure they complement the environment and enhance the over all ambiance. A majestic Mujalin tree, also known as the water jasmine, stands as the garden's centerpiece, surrounded by robust grapevines and stunning Australian Illawarra flame trees. Other plants include the striking variegated rubber plants and the tricolored leaves of the variegated tamarind, which blend pink, white, and green hues. Tropical plants like various Mons tera species, Bons, and ferns from around the world have been selected to cultivate the ultimatetropical atmosphere.
For the lighting, strategic placements accentuate the beauty of the garden at night. Tree spotlights highlight the plants' unique features after dusk, while the circular stage is illuminated by layered LED lights. White, minimalistic lamps spotlight the villa's white fences, and cylindrical white path lights cast a warm glow from above. Every detail has been intentionally chosen to make Villa de Amore a home of unforgettable memories for all who visit.

PARKING

The parking area at Villa de Amore offers a seamless blend of ecofriendly design and modern convenience, situated prominently at the villa's forefront. Upon arrival, guests are greeted by the villa's distinct logo, an emblem of the serene and luxurious experience that awaits within.
The parking lot utilizes square concrete blocks interspersed with lush grass, a design choice that harmonizes with the villa's commitment to natural beauty and sustainability. This "greenparking" approach allows for natural water drainage and reduces the heat island effect typically associated with conventional parking lots.
Designed to accommodate up to five vehicles directly in front of the villa, the parking lot ensures ample space for guests'cars. Additionally, there are two dedicated electric vehicle charging stations, reflecting the villa's forward-thinking amenities for eco-conscious travelers.
Should there be a need for additional parking, the villa has made provisions for extra space in close proximity, ensuring convenience for guests with more than five vehicles. This thoughtful planning ensures that even as the parking area maintains its aesthetic appeal, it does not compromise on functionality and guest comfort.

Facilities

  • 5 slots for car parking
  • 2 Ev Charger

BEACH ATTRACTION

Nestled in close proximity to the stunning Tawanron Beach, Villa de Amore offers the perfect retreat just a leisurely 700 meters' stroll away from this pristine coastal gem. Here, you will find a beach that embodies tranquility and natural beauty, away from the hustle and bustle of crowded shores.
Tawanron Beach boasts a serene ambiance, making it an ideal destination for those seeking a peaceful and rejuvenating escape. As you step onto the soft, powdery sands, you'll be greeted by the mesmerizing sight of the sun gracefully setting over the horizon. This picturesque moment offers the perfect backdrop for a tranquil and serene evening by the sea.
Whether you're looking to bask in the warm, golden rays of the sun, take a leisurely walk along the shore, or simply unwind while gazing at the breathtaking scenery, Tawanron Beach promises an idyllic and unspoiled coastal experience. Its unassuming charm and natural beauty make it the perfect spot to relax and immerse yourself in the soothing coastal atmosphere.
